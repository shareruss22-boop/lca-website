FAQ

Common Questions

How long does treatment take?

Most sessions take 60–90 minutes per person depending on hair length, thickness, and the level of infestation. We'll give you a more specific estimate when you reach out to schedule.

Do I need to do anything to prepare?

Come with dry, detangled hair and no product or conditioner in it. That's it — our technicians handle everything else. You don't need to bag stuffed animals or strip your whole house before coming in.

Is the treatment safe for children?

Yes — our method is completely chemical-free and safe for all ages, including newborns, pregnant women, and people with sensitive scalps. We do not use pesticide-based shampoos of any kind.

What if lice come back after treatment?

We stand behind our work. If lice return within the guarantee window after your treatment, we'll retreat at no additional charge. Just call us and we'll get you back in promptly.

Can you treat hair extensions, braids, or locs?

Yes. We offer a specialized alternative for clients with extensions, braids, locs, and other specialty hair situations where our standard process needs to be adapted. Our technicians will assess your specific situation and guide you through the best approach.

Should the whole family come in?

We recommend all immediate household members be checked — head checks are free. This helps prevent re-infestation and gives everyone peace of mind.
