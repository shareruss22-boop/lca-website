Getting the Most Out of Our Products

Tips from Our Technicians

Follow these steps for the best results at home.

1

Start with a Treatment Product

Apply the LCA Oil Treatment or Mousse Treatment to damp hair to immobilize lice and loosen nits from the hair shaft before combing.

2

Comb Thoroughly

Use the LCA Professional Lice Comb in small sections from root to tip. Wipe the comb clean between passes. Good lighting and a mirror are your best friends.

3

Prevent Re-Exposure

After treatment, switch to LCA Prevention Shampoo and Mint Spray as part of your daily routine to deter lice from coming back.
