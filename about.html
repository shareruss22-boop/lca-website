About Nicole

A Career Built on Care, Leadership & Community

Nicole Sanders is a dynamic entrepreneur, community advocate, and mother of three whose career blends innovation, service, and heart. With an early background in music — writing, arranging, and performing live — Nicole developed a foundation of discipline, creativity, and connection that continues to define her leadership today.

After relocating to Southern California, Nicole discovered the professional lice-removal industry and immediately recognized both its business potential and its life-changing impact on families. Through exceptional operational leadership, an uncompromising commitment to customer experience, and a natural ability to build and lead high-performing teams, she rose rapidly — ultimately becoming the owner and operator of multiple thriving clinics.

"I started in this industry as a technician and fell in love with helping families. There is nothing better than seeing the relief on a parent's face when we tell them — it's done."

Under Nicole's leadership, LCA has expanded across multiple states, serving families through both brick-and-mortar locations and mobile services. Her clinics are trusted partners to school districts, nurses, and community organizations, providing education, prevention programs, and rapid, compassionate care.

Philanthropy is central to Nicole's mission. She actively supports schools, donates services to families in need, and works to remove the stigma around scalp health through education and accessibility.

Our Origins

From One Clinic to a Nationwide Mission

LCA Lice Consultants & Associates was built on Nicole Sanders' vision to create the best lice removal company — one built on genuine care for families and community.

After relocating to San Diego and beginning her career at a lice removal company, Nicole's kind and courteous nature and her ability to truly listen to customers quickly made her the face of the organization. She recognized her talent for developing action plans and leading teams, earning multiple promotions along the way.

Seeing the Opportunity

Nicole saw firsthand how stressful and confusing lice infestations are for families — and how few quality options existed. She founded Lice Consultants & Associates to change that, starting in San Diego and growing to serve families coast-to-coast.

Growing with Purpose

Today LCA operates clinics in San Diego, Orlando, and York County, SC. Nicole continues expanding, driven by the same motivation she had on day one: to be a trusted, compassionate resource for every family dealing with lice.

Our Values

We know how stressful lice can be. Every client is treated with kindness, discretion, and zero judgment — because families deserve to feel supported, not shamed.

Science-Backed Method

Community First
