San Diego Lice Removal — FAQ

Answers to the questions we hear most from San Diego families

How much does lice removal cost in San Diego? +

Head Screening $30/person (free when a household member receives treatment). Full Air Treatment $220 long hair / $189 short hair (3" and under). Express Treatment $160/person. Manual Comb-Out $150/hr. Family discount available — call us for details.

Where is the LCA San Diego clinic? +

We're located at 5380 Clairemont Mesa Blvd, Suite 200, San Diego, CA 92117 — in the Clairemont Mesa area. Open Mon–Sat 8am–8pm, Sun 10am–2pm.

Do I need to do anything at home after treatment? +

No — treatment is 100% complete before you leave our clinic. No products to apply at home, no nit-picking homework, no bagging stuffed animals. Walk out and go back to normal life.

Is the treatment safe for my child? +

Yes. Our heated-air treatment is completely pesticide-free. It's safe for all ages — toddlers, school-age kids, teens, adults — as well as pregnant women and people with sensitive scalps.

How is LCA different from drugstore lice shampoos? +

Over-the-counter lice shampoos use pesticides that many lice strains have become resistant to. They often require multiple applications and leave nits behind. LCA uses heated air technology that physically kills lice and nits — no resistance possible, no chemicals, done in one visit.

Do you offer a guarantee? +

Yes. If re-exposure occurs within 30 days, you receive 50% off a re-treatment as a courtesy.

What products do you use? +

We use only all-natural, non-toxic products — no pesticides or harsh chemicals. Our in-clinic treatments use our LCA Oil Treatment and LCA Mousse Treatment paired with professional-grade metal lice combs.
