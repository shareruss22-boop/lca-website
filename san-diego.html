San Diego Lice Removal — FAQ

Answers to the questions we hear most from San Diego families

How much does lice removal cost in San Diego? +

Head Screening $30/person (free when a household member receives treatment). Full Air Treatment $220 long hair / $189 short hair (3" and under). Express Treatment $160/person. Manual Comb-Out $150/hr. Family discount available — call us for details.

Where is the LCA San Diego clinic? +

We're located at 5380 Clairemont Mesa Blvd, Suite 200, San Diego, CA 92117 — in the Clairemont Mesa area, easily accessible from I-805 and I-5. Open Mon–Sat 8am–8pm, Sun 10am–2pm.

Do I need to do anything at home after treatment? +

No — and this is what makes us different. Treatment is 100% complete before you leave our clinic. No products to apply at home, no nit-picking homework, no bagging stuffed animals. Walk out, go back to school or work the next day.

Is the treatment safe for my child? +

Yes. Our heated-air treatment is completely pesticide-free and FDA-cleared. It's safe for all ages — toddlers, school-age kids, teens, adults — as well as pregnant women and people with sensitive scalps. No chemicals touch your child's head.

How is LCA different from drugstore lice shampoos? +

Over-the-counter lice shampoos use pesticides (like permethrin or pyrethrin) that many lice strains have become resistant to. They often require multiple applications, leave nits behind, and put chemicals on your child's scalp. LCA uses heated air technology that physically kills lice and nits — no resistance possible, no chemicals, done in one visit.

Which areas of San Diego do you serve? +

Our Clairemont Mesa clinic serves all of San Diego County including La Jolla, Pacific Beach, Ocean Beach, Mission Valley, Mira Mesa, Kearny Mesa, Point Loma, North Park, Hillcrest, Carmel Valley, Del Mar, Solana Beach, Encinitas, Carlsbad, Rancho Bernardo, Poway, Santee, El Cajon, La Mesa, Chula Vista, and more.

Can you treat extensions, braids, or locs? +

Yes. Our Express Treatment is specifically designed for clients with extensions, braids, locs, or other specialty hair where our standard heated-air process needs to be adapted. Our technicians will assess your specific situation and recommend the best approach.

Do you offer a guarantee? +

Yes. If lice return within the guarantee period after your treatment, we'll re-treat at no additional charge. Additionally, if re-exposure occurs within 30 days, you receive 50% off a re-treatment as a courtesy.
