Absolutely. Our method is 100% pesticide-free, using professional heated-air technology that’s safe for children of all ages. No harsh chemicals, no medicated shampoos — just a safe, effective, FDA-cleared treatment.

Do I need to do anything at home after treatment? +

No. Treatment is completed entirely during your clinic visit. There’s nothing to apply, no products to use at home, and no follow-up appointments needed. You leave lice-free.

What is your re-exposure policy? +

If you’re re-exposed to lice within 30 days of treatment, we’ll re-treat at 50% off ($97.50 for a Full Air Treatment). We also offer a 10-day guarantee — any issues within 10 days and we’ll take care of it.
