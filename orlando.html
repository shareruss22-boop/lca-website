Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about mobile lice removal in Orlando

How much does lice removal cost in Orlando? +

Head Screening: $30/person (free when someone in the household receives treatment). Full Air Treatment: $170 long hair / $140 short hair (3” and under). Express Treatment: $160/person. Manual Comb-Out: $150/hr. All mobile appointments include a $50 travel fee.

Do you come to my home in Orlando? +

Yes — LCA Orlando is a mobile-only service. Our certified technician comes directly to your home anywhere in the Orlando metro area, bringing all professional equipment. No clinic visit, no driving, no waiting rooms.

Is there a travel fee? +

Yes, a $50 travel fee applies to all mobile appointments in the Orlando area. This covers the cost of our technician traveling to your home with all professional heated-air equipment.

Do you offer same-day appointments in Orlando? +

Yes — we offer same-day and next-day appointments subject to availability. Call us or book online and we’ll confirm your appointment time within an hour during business hours.

Is the treatment safe for young children? +

Absolutely. Our method is 100% pesticide-free, using professional heated-air technology that is safe for children of all ages. No harsh chemicals, no shampoos — just a safe, effective, FDA-cleared treatment.

What areas around Orlando do you serve? +

We serve all of Orlando and surrounding communities including Winter Park, Kissimmee, Sanford, Lake Mary, Altamonte Springs, Longwood, Casselberry, Oviedo, Windermere, Dr. Phillips, Celebration, Clermont, Apopka, Winter Garden, Maitland, Deltona, and the Daytona Beach area. Not sure if we cover you? Give us a call.

Can you treat our whole family in one visit? +

Yes — we can treat multiple family members in a single mobile appointment. Head screenings are free for anyone in the household when another family member receives treatment.

What is your re-exposure guarantee? +

We offer a 10-day guarantee — if you have any issues within 10 days of treatment, we’ll take care of it. Plus, if you’re re-exposed to lice within 30 days, we’ll return and re-treat at 50% off.
